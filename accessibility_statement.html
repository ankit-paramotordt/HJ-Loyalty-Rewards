Accessibility Statement

Accessibility Statement of HJ Reward Limited
Effective Date: September 17, 2025
This Accessibility Statement (“Statement”) sets forth the ongoing commitment of HJ Reward Limited (“HJ Reward”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) to accessibility, inclusion, and equal opportunity in the delivery of our digital services and platforms. It is intended to provide transparent information regarding the measures we have implemented, and continue to implement, to ensure that persons with disabilities can access, navigate, and benefit from our website, mobile applications, and associated digital resources (collectively, the “Site”).

1. Purpose and Scope

1.1. The purpose of this Statement is to set out HJ Reward’s approach to accessibility and the standards to which we aspire regarding digital inclusion.
1.2. This Statement applies to all digital properties operated and maintained by HJ Reward, including but not limited to websites, web applications, and mobile applications, irrespective of the device or platform used to access them.
1.3. Accessibility is integral to our mission to provide all users, regardless of ability or circumstance, with fair and equal access to our services.

2. Commitment to Accessibility

2.1. HJ Reward affirms its commitment to making the Site accessible to the widest possible audience, including but not limited to persons with visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor disabilities.
2.2. We view accessibility not only as a compliance obligation but as a core company value, reflecting our dedication to inclusivity, diversity, and equal opportunity.
2.3. Our digital properties are developed, maintained, and updated in reference to internationally recognized accessibility standards, specifically the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).
2.4. We recognize that accessibility is a continuous journey requiring regular review, adaptation, and improvement as standards, technologies, and user needs evolve.

3. Accessibility Measures and Practices

3.1. To achieve and maintain accessibility, HJ Reward integrates inclusive design principles throughout the lifecycle of our digital platforms.
3.2. Our accessibility measures include, but are not limited to, the following:
3.2.1. Conducting both automated and manual accessibility testing to proactively identify and remedy barriers encountered by users with disabilities.
3.2.2. Providing text alternatives (alt text) for all non-text content to ensure that users employing assistive technologies can access equivalent information.
3.2.3. Ensuring that all features and functions of the Site are operable via keyboard navigation, without requiring mouse input.
3.2.4. Implementing sufficient colour contrast, clear font readability, and scalable text options to enhance visibility and legibility across devices and user preferences.
3.2.5. Training team members, including developers and content creators, as well as third-party service providers, to integrate accessibility best practices into all aspects of design, development, and maintenance.
3.2.6. Regularly reviewing the Site to monitor for accessibility compliance and make necessary updates.
3.2.7. Utilizing accessible forms, error identification, and feedback mechanisms to support user engagement and interaction.
3.2.8. Integrating ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) attributes where appropriate to improve compatibility with assistive technologies.
3.3. While we strive to adhere to accepted guidelines and standards for accessibility and usability, it is not always possible to do so in all areas of the Site. We are committed to ongoing evaluation and to addressing any accessibility issues identified.

4. Third-Party Content and Technology

4.1. Our Site may contain links to external websites, third-party applications, or embedded content that are not directly controlled by HJ Reward.
4.2. While we endeavor to ensure that such third-party content is accessible, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of digital properties outside our direct control.
4.3. We will, wherever feasible, encourage third-party providers to follow accessibility best practices consistent with our own standards.

5. Feedback and Contact Information

5.1. We welcome and value feedback as a critical component in our ongoing accessibility efforts.
5.2. Should you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact our Accessibility Team using the following methods:
Email: accessibility@hjreward.com
Postal Address: Level 1, Maeva Tower, Corner Silicon and Bank Streets, Ebene Cyber City 72201, Republic of Mauritius.
5.3. When contacting us, please provide as much detail as possible regarding the nature of the accessibility problem, the page or feature involved, and your contact information so that we may respond promptly and effectively.
5.4. We endeavor to respond to accessibility-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe and to provide, where possible, accessible solutions or alternative means of access to requested content or services.

6. Continuous Improvement and Review

6.1. HJ Reward is committed to the ongoing review and enhancement of digital accessibility across our platforms.
6.2. We will continue to monitor developments in legal, technological, and industry accessibility standards, and to update our policies and practices as necessary to remain current and effective.
6.3. Periodic audits and user testing will be conducted to assess the effectiveness of our accessibility measures and to identify opportunities for further improvement.
6.4. We recognize that user needs may change over time, and we will consult with experts, organizations representing persons with disabilities, and our user community to ensure that our accessibility efforts remain responsive and relevant.

7. Enforcement and Accountability

7.1. HJ Reward takes accessibility seriously and maintains internal procedures for reporting, investigating, and remediating accessibility issues.
7.2. All employees and contractors with responsibilities for the Site are expected to comply with this Statement and to act in accordance with our commitment to accessibility.
7.3. Breaches of this Statement may result in corrective action, up to and including the removal of noncompliant content, retraining, or review of relevant policies and procedures.

8. Legal Rights and Compliance

8.1. This Statement is intended to demonstrate HJ Reward’s good faith efforts to comply with applicable laws, including but not limited to the Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities, and relevant national and regional accessibility legislation.
8.2. Nothing in this Statement is intended to limit or waive any rights or remedies available under applicable law.

9. Modifications to this Statement

9.1. HJ Reward reserves the right to amend or update this Accessibility Statement at any time to reflect changes in our practices or to remain in compliance with evolving legal requirements.
9.2. The “Effective Date” indicated at the top of this Statement will be updated to reflect the date of the most recent revision.

10. Additional Assistance

10.1. If you require information from the Site in an alternative format or need additional assistance, please contact us using the methods outlined in Section 5.
10.2. We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate requests for accessible formats and communication supports in a timely manner.
