Cookie Policy

Effective Date: August 19, 2025

This Cookie Policy outlines how HJ Reward Limited (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, mobile applications, and related platforms (collectively referred to as the “Site”) in connection with the HJ Reward Program (the “Program”). By accessing or using the Site or participating in the Program, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone, etc.) when you visit our Site. They enable the Site to recognize your device, track your interactions, and improve your user experience. Cookies can be either session-based, which are temporary and expire once you close your browser, or persistent, which remain on your device for a set period.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies to enhance your experience with the Site. Strictly necessary cookies are essential for the operation of the Site, such as enabling you to navigate and access secure areas. Performance cookies collect data on how you use the Site, which allows us to improve its functionality and address technical issues. Functionality cookies allow the Site to remember your preferences, such as language or login information, to offer a more personalized experience. Targeting or advertising cookies track your browsing habits across websites to deliver ads tailored to your interests. Lastly, we may use third-party cookies, placed by our service providers, for analytics and advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are used on our website for multiple purposes, including enhancing the user experience, personalizing content, and providing targeted advertisements. They also help us analyze usage patterns and improve Site functionality. Additionally, cookies are utilized to ensure the security of our services, prevent fraud, and maintain the integrity of the Program.

4. Managing and Controlling Cookies

You have the ability to control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that blocking or deleting cookies may impact your ability to use certain features of the Site. Most modern browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences, including disabling certain cookies or clearing your browsing history. You may also choose to opt-out of targeted advertising by visiting industry-standard opt-out tools.

5. Privacy and Data Protection

We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal data. The information collected through cookies is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy, which details how we handle personal data. We do not share information collected through cookies with unauthorized third parties. Any personal data collected via cookies is used solely for the purposes of improving your experience with our Program and services, and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update or amend this Cookie Policy as necessary. Changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
