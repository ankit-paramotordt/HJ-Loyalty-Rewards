About HJ Loyalty & Rewards

What is HJ Loyalty & Rewards?
HJ Loyalty & Rewards is a multi-partner programme rewarding customers for purchases across our partners in Mauritius. It rewards customers with points which can be used to buy products and services at participating partners. Members also receive discounts from our discount partners.
→ Please stay connected by email, on Facebook or on the app: https://wiivrewards.com/wiiv-app
What are partners?
Our partners are the brands and retailers who make up the backbone of the programme—some of Mauritius’s most loved brands. Partners let you earn points when you shop at eligible earn partners, and enjoy those points (spend to save) at eligible enjoy partners. We also have partners that give guaranteed discounts every time you shop.
View the list of partners directly in the app.
* To benefit from Ala-lila and Espace Maison as partners, members must also be a member of those programmes (Ala-lila; Club Espace Maison).

Join

How much does it cost?

It’s absolutely free! There is no cost to join HJ Loyalty & Rewards.

Who is eligible to join?
To join you must:
• be at least 18 years old
• reside in Mauritius
• hold a valid NIC or resident’s permit number in Mauritius.
Is there any change in terms & conditions?

Yes, they may change from time to time. Please review the latest Terms & Conditions on the app or website.

How do I join the new programme?

There are 3 ways to join:

  1. Quick enrol at the cashier counter of any Winners store. Complete your enrolment via the app or Winners consigne within 72 hours to keep your account and points.
  2. In-store at participating registration outlets: any Winners consigne and specific MedActiv outlets*.
  3. Download our app on Google Play, iOS App Store or Huawei App Gallery.

* Registration at MedActiv is available at La Chaussée and Phoenix Mall only.

Is there any incentive for signing up?

Yes—new members enjoy bonus points and exclusive discounts at multiple partners. See details on our website and mobile app, and follow us on social for updates.

What is the quick enrolment?

The fastest route to becoming a member. At any Winners till, provide your NIC and mobile number to start earning immediately.

What happens to my account and data once quick enrolled?

Your NIC and mobile are stored in our system; you start earning points immediately. You’ll receive an SMS with your digital membership number and a One-Time Password (OTP).

How long do I have to do the full registration?

You have 72 hours to complete full enrolment using the OTP. Otherwise your account, personal data and any accumulated points will be deleted automatically.

I didn’t receive the OTP. What should I do?
Possible reasons:
(i) Network issue — ask the cashier to resend the OTP.
(ii) Wrong mobile or NIC/RP captured — contact Customer Service on 203-2222 or 460-7920, or email contactus@wiivrewards.com quickly to correct and complete within 72 hours.
What information is required during full enrolment?

Your valid NIC or RP number, mobile number and email address. If you quick enrolled, you’ll also need the OTP sent by SMS.

Why do I have to provide personal information?
We need it to identify you, protect your points, and let some partners authenticate you via NIC/RP. We take data security seriously—see our Grievance Redressal Policy Policy (available in the app or website footer).
If I join through one partner, do I need to join the others?
No—one membership number (digital or physical card) works across partners.
• Ala-lila: register on their app/site and add your member ID.
• Espace Maison: join CEM app and add your secret code (call 203-2222 or 460-7920 if you need help).
Do I have to accept the Terms & Conditions if I was a Fidelity member?
Yes. It ensures everyone understands benefits, proper use, and data practices (as required by the Mauritian Data Protection Act).

IBL Employees

I used to be an IBL employee. Do I still get benefits?

If you’re no longer an IBL employee, HR/HC will update your status to consumer. You’ll keep enjoying consumer benefits and offers.

Member Card

Where do I get a card?

Access your digital card in the app (Google Play, iOS App Store, Huawei). New members get it immediately upon registration.

What if I don’t have a smartphone?
We no longer issue physical cards by default; exceptions may be granted case-by-case. You can still earn/redeem by presenting your NIC at Winners and MedActiv, and earn at Eagle Insurance (ensure your NIC is correctly registered). For discount partners, a physical card may be required—request via contactus@wiivrewards.com or call the numbers above.
I’m unfamiliar with apps—can you help?

Visit any Winners consigne or our office at Caudan for help, or call 203-2222 / 460-7920 and we’ll guide you.

I lost my card. Can I get another one?
Freeze your account in the app or by calling support to protect your points. Then request a new digital membership number via 203-2222 / 460-7920 or contactus@wiivrewards.com. Physical cards are exceptional; from the second replacement onward, a Rs 250 fee may apply.

Earning Points

How do I earn points?

Present your card (digital/physical) or NIC/RP at earn partner locations when making eligible purchases. See Terms & Conditions for full rules.

When can I start earning?

Immediately after quick enrolment at Winners tills or once registered in-store or on the app.

Where can I earn points?

At Winners, MedActiv, Eagle Insurance and Ala-lila.

How many points do I earn at each partner?

Winners

  • 1 point for every Rs 200 qualifying spend (excl. promotional items & non-qualifying purchases)
  • 5% in points on Leaderprice & Casino products (excl. promotional items)
  • Exclusive bonus points on hundreds of products daily
  • Scan-to-Pay: +1 bonus point per Rs 200 spent digitally

MedActiv

  • 1 point per Rs 200 qualifying spend in store

Eagle Insurance

  • 2 points per Rs 100 qualifying spend on eligible policies bought directly

Ala-lila

  • 4 points per Rs 100 qualifying spend on rides (add your member ID in their app)
Can I earn points and still get partner discounts?

Yes—exclusively at Eagle Insurance. Points are based on the premium after discounts.

How long before points appear?

Typically within 24–72 hours; in some cases up to 45 days.

What if systems are offline?

During outages/upgrades, allocations/discounts won’t be processed retrospectively by default. Some partners may allocate after the fact at their discretion if you were authenticated.

Any items excluded from earning?
• Winners: tobacco, gas, prepaid mobile cards, dépôt (consignee), packaging, delivery charges.
• Eagle Insurance: medical, travel and commercial policies.
House brands on instant discount/bonus points promos don’t earn base points.
Can I earn and spend in the same purchase?

Yes—you’ll still earn as per rules even if you pay partly with points.

Do I earn on totals including VAT?

Yes—on the net total including VAT, excluding promo items/bonus-point items and non-qualifying purchases.

Do I need to keep till receipts?

Not for earning, but keep them for returns or to resolve discrepancies. Your receipt (with your card number) helps support investigations.

Can I earn when shopping Winners online?

Yes—the same earn rules apply.

Spending Points

How do I spend my points?

Redeem at Winners and MedActiv. Present your card (digital/physical) or NIC. Cashiers may verify your NIC.

Where can I spend?

At Winners and MedActiv. Present your card or NIC at checkout.

Minimum points to spend?

Minimum 1 point. 1 point = Rs 1.

Can I spend points on Winners online?

Not yet—you can earn, but cannot redeem online for now.

What if I don’t have enough points?

Combine points with cash/debit/credit per partner payment rules.

Can I convert points to cash?

No, but you can pay with points in-store (1 point = Rs 1).

Can I share points with others?

No, points aren’t transferable.

Can someone else use my card?

No—your account is linked to your NIC; each person should join for free to earn their own rewards.

Is redemption secured?

Yes—redemptions of 200+ points require an SMS OTP. Keep your mobile number updated in your profile.

My Points Balance

How can I check my points?
• In the app (Home or Transactions)
• Ask the cashier at Winners or MedActiv
• Authenticate at any Winners consigne or via the call centre
Missing points?

Some partner transactions can take up to 45 days. Check your app history or call 203-2222 / 460-7920. Retro-allocations aren’t guaranteed. Inactivity for 12 months deactivates your account and points expire.

Returns & exchanges

Returned items may deduct earned points; points used for purchases may be re-credited—policies vary by partner. See Terms & Conditions for details.

Do points expire?

Yes—after 12 months of inactivity (no earn or redeem). Your account and data are then permanently deleted (irreversible).

Partner Discounts

Which partners give discounts?

Many! Check the app or website for current deals and guaranteed discounts.

How do I get the discount?

Present your digital/physical card. Offers vary by partner and product. See the “Partners / Save” pages or wiiv-benefits.

Save offers vs special deals?

Save partners = guaranteed discounts year-round. Special deals = ad-hoc promos. We’ll email/SMS these—ensure your profile is up to date and you’ve opted in.

How to benefit at Espace Maison?

Get 10% off on specific monthly deals via the CEM app. You must join CEM and enter your secret code. Need help? Call 203-2222 / 460-7920.

Can I swipe my card/app at Espace Maison?

No, use your Club Espace Maison (CEM) digital card within their app.

My Account & Profile

How do I check or update my details?
• In the app: My Profile
• Visit a Winners consigne, MedActiv La Chaussée or Phoenix Mall, or our office (you may need to verify and accept T&Cs)
• Call 203-2222 / 460-7920 or email contactus@wiivrewards.com
Don’t know how to use the app?

Call our helpdesk at 203-2222 or 460-7920 to enquire about your account.

What if I never use the programme?

After 12 months of inactivity, points expire and your account/data are deleted. We’ll send a reminder one month prior.

I forgot my password/username

Use “Forgot Password?” in the app, entering your NIC/RP and registered mobile. If issues persist, call 203-2222 / 460-7920 or email us.

I want to cancel my account

You can request deletion in the app (More → Delete my account) or via the call centre. Deletion is irreversible and removes all data and points.

Can I opt out of marketing and still get offers?

An email is required for membership. You can opt out of marketing in emails or in the app (My Profile). You’ll still receive essential notifications.

What will my information be used for?

To verify identity, protect your account/points, and personalize offers. See our Grievance Redressal Policy Policy for full details.
