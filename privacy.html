2. Categories of Personal Data Collected

To provide the Program and associated services, we collect different types of personal data directly from you or automatically through your interactions with our website, mobile application, and other Program-related channels. The categories of personal data we may collect include the following:

2.1 Personal Data Provided by You

When you register for the Program or use our services, we collect the following personal data:

  • Personal Identification Information: Full name, date of birth, and contact details, including postal address, email address, and phone number.
  • Account Information: Membership ID, account credentials, security verification data, and any additional data required to manage your Program membership.
  • Transactional Data: Information about your transactions, including the accrual and redemption of HJ Points, program interactions, and rewards claims.
  • Marketing Preferences: Your preferences with respect to receiving marketing communications, promotional offers, and Program updates.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

We also collect data automatically when you interact with our website or mobile application. This may include:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system version, and other information about the device or platform used to access our services.
  • Usage Data: Data regarding your activity on our website, mobile app, and other platforms, including page visits, clicks, and interaction with Program features.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Information obtained through the use of cookies, analytics tools, and other tracking technologies (refer to our Cookie Policy for more details).

3. ATM, Credit Cards and Payment Information

When you purchase points through the website or mobile application, you are required to select a method of payment. HJ Reward is not interested in your debit/credit card information nor do we store any of your debit/credit information. For transactions with online debit/credit cards, we transmit your entire card information to the appropriate debit/credit card company in an encrypted format with globally accepted rules and applications during order processing. Upon your choice, we keep a part of your card information in an encrypted format, taking precaution to maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards over your credit card information.

  • To provide assistance with account inquiries, transaction issues, or other customer service matters.
  • To send service updates, promotional offers, and Program-related information (only where legally permitted).
  • To fulfill our legal obligations, including compliance with applicable laws and regulations governing data protection, fraud prevention, and financial reporting.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities that may affect the Program and its Members.
  • For business analysis, planning, and performance evaluation to improve the quality and functionality of the Program.

5. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • The processing is necessary to perform our obligations under the Program’s Terms and Conditions, including facilitating the accrual and redemption of HJ Points.

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by applicable law. Once the retention period has expired, your personal data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

As a Member, you have the following rights regarding your personal data, subject to applicable laws:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request corrections or updates to inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal or contractual obligations).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit the processing of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If processing is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority if you believe that your data protection rights have been violated.

Requests to exercise these rights can be made by contacting us at privacy@hjreward.com.

HJ Reward is committed to ensuring the security, integrity, and protection of Member accounts and Programme data.
