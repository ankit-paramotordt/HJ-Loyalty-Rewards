Privacy Notice for HJ Loyalty & Rewards

Last updated: 15 April 2024

1. Introduction

IBL Ltd (BRN: C07001778) (“IBL”, “we”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and to ensure that your personal data is collected and processed lawfully. As a result, we would like to inform you regarding the way we would use and process your personal data when you become a member of IBL Loyalty program (“HJ Loyalty & Rewards”, “the Programme”) as defined in the Terms and Conditions.

We recommend that you read this Privacy Notice (hereafter the “Notice”) so that you understand our approach towards the use of your personal data. Should your personal data be processed other than for the purposes of HJ Loyalty & Rewards, you will be required to give your consent to such processing.

If you do not agree with these terms, you choose not to provide your personal data and this will impact on our ability to enroll you with HJ Loyalty & Rewards. This Notice forms part of our Terms and Conditions and such shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the relevant laws.

This Notice has been designed in compliance with the provisions of the Mauritius Data Protection Act 2017 (hereafter the “MDPA”).

2. Our Services

IBL provides HJ Loyalty & Rewards (as defined in the Terms & Conditions) through its website https://www.wiivrewards.com (“Website”) and the software application installed on your smartphone (“App”).

When you use the 'Quick Enroll' method to register to HJ Loyalty & Rewards, which is the first step in the registration process, your NIC and mobile number, along with purchase details and points earned during this initial transaction, are stored in the programme’s database for 72 hours. If the member completes the full enrollment process to become a confirmed member, their personal data and points are stored until they choose to delete their account. However, if the member does not complete the full enrollment within the specified 72-hour period, their collected personal data, purchase details, and earned points are permanently deleted from our system and database.

Therefore, once you’re registered and use the membership card, you will earn points or discounts off products and services at selected Partners. By using the card, you also give us the opportunity to learn more about your purchase habits and offer you personalised information and/or promotions. When you redeem points worth 200 points or more, you authorize us to send you a one-time-password (OTP) as a security measure. This OTP must then be provided to the cashier to complete the transaction.

By accepting the Terms and Conditions and this Privacy Notice of HJ Loyalty & Rewards, you also consent to the processing of your personal data as described in this section.

3. Collection of Personal data

We collect and process your personal data mainly upon registration to provide you with access to our Services, to support our contractual relationship with you and while using your membership card as part of the Programme. In certain instances, we may process your personal data for certain other purposes as detailed below and for this, your consent shall be sought beforehand.

We collect personal information of members of the Programme while they use their card at the Partners’ outlets, via an external registration loyalty cloud platform & administration system.

Types of personal data:

  • Name, surname and title - Mandatory upon registration
  • Date of birth - Mandatory upon registration
  • Email address - Mandatory upon registration
  • Mobile number - Mandatory upon registration
  • National Identity Card number or Residential Permit number - Mandatory upon registration
  • City of residential location - Mandatory upon registration
  • Number of children - Optional
  • Member purchasing preference - Optional
  • Purchase details (products, prices and outlets) - Mandatory upon use of card
  • Membership number - Mandatory upon registration and delivery of card
  • Points balances - Mandatory upon use of card

5. How we use your data

We will use your personal data only for the purposes for which it was collected or agreed with you, for instance:

  • To render our Services as defined in Terms and Conditions;
  • To send you One-Time-Passwords (OTP) on your mobile phone, for verification and security purposes;
  • To carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you and us pursuant to the Services;
  • To notify you about changes to our Services; to respond to queries or comments;
  • To allow IBL Companies and Programme Partners to contact you in relation with service rendered; to assist your queries via the call centre;
  • To inform you about downtime, new features or promotions; competitions or promotions;
  • To collect device data (e.g., IP, browser/OS) to improve your experience; evaluate site usage; audit/records; market research; monitoring;
  • To personalise your experience and suggest products/services (including relevant third parties) where permitted;
  • For business development of Partners; customer satisfaction research; statistical analysis;
  • To confirm and verify identity for security purposes;
  • To analyse purchase habits and provide relevant information or promotions; and to comply with legal/regulatory requirements.

Profiling and automated decision making: We analyse purchasing patterns and certain personal details to send targeted marketing (sometimes automatically), run specific promotions for selected clients, and manage tier benefits. You can opt out of marketing at any time using the methods we provide.

6. Retention of your personal data

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or for legitimate business/legal reasons. Residual copies may persist in backups for a limited time.

All Member Registration Data will be deleted 1 year after termination of your membership or 1 year after the account has been classified inactive. All Transactional Data will be anonymised and kept for statistics and data integrity.

7. Disclosure of personal data

We may disclose your personal data to business partners involved in delivering our Services (loyalty platform providers, Programme Partners, analysts, customer services, call centre provider, outsourced promoters) and IBL Group companies (e.g., to manage employee/consumer status).

Access is limited to designated persons on a need-to-know basis; third parties are contractually obliged to safeguard data. We may also disclose where required by law or to authorities, or if unlawful activities are suspected. Aggregate/de-identified data may be shared.

8. Transfer of personal data abroad

We may transfer personal data outside Mauritius with appropriate safeguards aligned to the MDPA. Registration & administration platform hosting is in Germany.

Information you publish on our website may be accessible globally; we cannot prevent misuse by others. You expressly agree to the transfers described in this section.

9. Processing must be justified

We process data based on: performance of a contract; your consent; legal obligations; or our legitimate interests as a commercial organisation.

We employ reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data and confidentiality, subject to ongoing review. We cannot guarantee uninterrupted website operation and aren’t liable for issues inherent to electronic communications.

11. Children and Minors

We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a minor, use our website and services only with parent/guardian permission.

12. Links to other websites

13. Access to your personal data

14. Correction of your personal data

You may ask us to update, correct or delete your personal data. We’ll verify identity before making changes. Please keep your data up to date by notifying us of changes.

We will verify your identity and aim to respond within 30 days where reasonable. We may refuse or charge a fee in certain circumstances, or retain data where required by law or for legal claims.
